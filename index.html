Concept of the project

Irish Platinum is a flagship luxury project that is coming up in sector 10 of Greater Noida West. The project received the RERA certificate on 02/03/2024 and the work on the project has started. Main theme behind the project is to provide a high-end apartments with best construction quality. Sector 10 is one of the best sectors of Greater Noida west with high end projects like Coco-county, ATS Homecrakt, Mahagun Mantra. Sector is on 130 mtr road and has a wide green belt of 50 meters on the entry road. Sector 10 is surrounded by plots and low rise apartments and lots of open space.
RERA NO.: UPRERAPRJ503189/03/2024

Layout of the project

Project Irish platinum will have four towers, two towers of 3 BHK apartment and two towers of 4 BHK apartments. Project will be podium based where all facilities will be on an elevated platform, movement of vehicles will he restricted to basement and ground area. Construction of the flats will be done by MIVAN technology and all specifications of the project will be high-end. 85% area in the project will be green and the club house and swimming pool will be between the two towers of 3BHK.

Basic Features of project Irish Platinum

Irish Platinum is a luxury project with high end specifications. All towers will have a entrance lobby of 22 feet. All apartments will have ceiling height of 11 feet with all doors of around 8 feet high. Minimum size of the apartment will be 1390 sq ft with a configuration of 3 rooms and 2 bathrooms. The size of the flat goes up to 2550 sq having 4 rooms, 5 bathrooms, Pooja room and servant room. Apartments in Irish Platinum will have 8 feet wide balconies having glass facade and stainless-steel supports. Construction of the flat will be done through latest MIVAN technology.

Tower Clusters of Irish Platinum

In the project there will be four towers, with two clusters of 3 BHK flats and two clusters of 4 BHK flats. 3 BHK cluster will have 6 flats with two units of 1390 sq ft, two units of 1690 sq ft and two units of 1925 sq ft. All units will be three side open and apartments will be sharing maximum one all with adjacent apartment. Gap between two such tower clusters will be around 80 ft.
4 BHK cluster will have 4 flats with two units of 2150 sq ft and two units of 2550 sq ft. All units will be three side open and apartments will not be sharing any wall with any adjacent apartment. Gap between two apartments will be around 30 feet. Gap between two tower clusters will be around 100 ft.

Luxury Features

Project concept and planning is done be one of the top architect of India. The towers and apartments are made in a manner that there will be maximum gaps between the same. Following luxury features will be provided in the project:-

  • Double height five star entrance lobby

Mivan Construction

The project will be constructed with MIVAN technology with specially ordered MIVNA shuttering that will be imported into India. MIVAN technology is the most popular construction technology and is highly favoured by the home buyers. This is evident with the fact that the going rate of the flats constructed by MIVAN technology in this area is 30-40% higher in cost that those constructed by other technology. Advantage of such construction is better quality, long lasting structure, lesser problems like seepage etc. Moreover, the construction looks smooth and well structured.

Elevated Podium

Elevated podium in the project Irish Platinum will provide segregation of common area from the area earmarked for movement of vehicles. The podium will have car parking below and will have gardens on the top. It will help in achieving the following:

  • Safety to family specially children
  • Add on to beauty of the project
  • Lesser sound and air pollution because of the traffic
  • Smooth movement of vehicles
  • Easily accessible car parking

Questions & Answers

Starting price of a flat in Irish platinum project is around Rs 1.20 crores for a 1390 sq ft unit and it goes up to Rs 2.80 crores for 2550 sq ft apartment. This is an inaugural price for first 50 buyers. The average price of a flat in the project is around Rs 9000/- per sq ft, this price is likely to go up by Rs 1000/- per sq ft once first 50 flats are sold. As of now there are very few flats of size 1390 sq ft and 1690 sq ft available for sale. For exact price of a flat one can refer to the price list or the calculation sheet provided in the website.

As per the RERA documents possession date of Irish Platinum project is 61 months from the date of RERA certification, as the RERA certificate was given to the builder on 02 March 2024, the likely completion date of the project is 01 Apr 2029, however, looking at the track record of the builder and considering that the project is made on MIVAN technology (which is much faster in terms of speed of construction), the project is likely to be completed by 01 Apr 2028.

Presently the construction of first two towers are under progress. Also the construction of experience centre and sales Pavalion is going on. Pilling and basement work of first two towers is likely to be completed in next six months

Extra charges on the project Irish Platinum is Rs 500 per sq ft, Rs 2 lac for extra car parking and Rs 200 as lease rent and IFMS. The GST charges will be 5% of the cost of the basic price and 18% on the cost of other charges. The registration charges of the flat will be 6% post possession of the flat. For more details please check the price list or the calculation sheet posted on our website.

Irish Platinum is a luxury project with best specifications that one can expect. Likely price of the project on possession is likely to be Rs 15000/- per sq ft. One can invest in the project with investment horizon of around four years. As the payment to made will go in instalments and one can also take loan from bank, the likely returns on investment will be good. However, short term investors should stay away from this project.
